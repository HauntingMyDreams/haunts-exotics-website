About Us

Founded by a hobbyist new to the world of bioactive enclosures, Haunts Crawlers was created out of a passion for learning and a desire to share that experience with others. Whether you're just starting out or expanding your collection, the goal is to provide quality organisms and help others enjoy and succeed in the hobby.

We specialize in bioactive clean-up crews, with plans to expand into other exotic invertebrates, including roaches and additional species suited for thriving ecosystems.

Why Choose Haunts Crawlers?


  • Carefully bred, healthy cultures
  • Beginner-friendly guidance
  • Local pickup for safe, stress-free transfer
  • Passion-driven hobbyist operation
